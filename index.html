Trip to Southeast Asia

In the summer of 2019, I traveled to the opposite side of the planet to visit my partner at the time and her family. After 24 hours of airplane travel, I arrived in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Here are some snippets of my trip!

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

This is where I spent three weeks. I spent a lot of time at the malls and parks and I visited a petting zoo where I got to pet and feed racoons! Just outside the city, I visited Batu Caves, a Hindu temple set in limestone caves, where I got to interact with wild monkeys! I spent a day at Genting Highlands, aka the Las Vegas of Asia. It is a hill station city located on the peak of Mount Ulu Kali at 1,800 meters elevation. It is also where the world's largest hotel is located.

Penang Island

I spent a weekend at a beautiful beach resort. The island is known for its truly amazing food. We also walked around to shop at the night markets.

Phuket, Thailand

I only spent a weekend in Thailand but this was the best time of my life. We rode on speedboats and spent a day island hopping. I also ate delicious food, received an amazing foot massage, and even experienced a fish pedicure.
